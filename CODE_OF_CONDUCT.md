# Code of Conduct

Captain Cool follows a simple contributor standard: be respectful, specific, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Keep discussions focused on the work and the user experience.
- Give actionable feedback with enough context to reproduce or improve the issue.
- Respect that this is a learning/demo repository and prioritize clarity over gatekeeping.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing another person's secrets, credentials, or private data.
- Spam, trolling, or knowingly misleading reports.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this standard.
